About Lone Mountain Vistas

Nestled against the majestic backdrop of Lone Mountain in northwest Las Vegas, Lone Mountain Vistas offers an unparalleled living experience that combines luxury with natural beauty.

Our exclusive community features custom-designed homes, spacious lots, and world-class amenities, all while providing convenient access to Las Vegas' best shopping, dining, and entertainment.

At an elevation of approximately 3,000 feet, Lone Mountain provides a cooler microclimate than much of the Las Vegas Valley, with homes positioned to maximize views of both the mountain and the glittering city lights.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Lone Mountain Vistas

Homes in Lone Mountain Vistas typically range from $900,000 to $2.5 million, depending on size, location, views, and features. Our current inventory includes properties from 2,600 to 4,800 square feet.

Lone Mountain Vistas is served by the Clark County School District, with Lone Mountain Elementary School, Ernest Becker Middle School, and Centennial High School as the zoned public schools. Several top-rated private schools are also within a short drive.

Residents enjoy access to community trails, parks, and recreation areas. Many homes feature private pools, outdoor kitchens, and entertainment spaces. The community is minutes from Lone Mountain Regional Park, which offers hiking trails, sports fields, and picnic areas.

Lone Mountain Vistas is approximately 15-20 minutes (12 miles) from the Las Vegas Strip, offering the perfect balance of proximity to world-class entertainment while maintaining a peaceful, suburban atmosphere.

Property taxes in Clark County, Nevada are among the lowest in the country, typically ranging from 0.5% to 0.75% of the assessed value. Nevada has no state income tax, making it an attractive option for homebuyers.
